# YTP Brand Voice — Master Reference
*Your Trade Partner — Australian Trades Growth Agency*

---

## Who We Are
Your Trade Partner (YTP) is a growth agency built exclusively for Australian tradies — plumbers, electricians, roofers, painters, landscapers, builders, HVAC, concreters. We help them stop missing jobs online and capture every enquiry.

**We are not a marketing company.** We are a practical partner who understands the tools, the hours, the quoting, and the pressure of running a trade business.

---

## Target Audience
- Australian tradies running their own business (1–10 employees)
- Time-poor, skeptical of marketing fluff, direct communicators
- Main pain points: missed calls, losing jobs to competitors online, no time to manage their digital presence, wasted quoting time
- They respond to: plain talk, specific numbers, practical fixes, no corporate language

---

## Voice & Tone

**Sound like:** A straight-talking mate who knows the trades inside-out and has seen what works.

**Always:**
- Short, punchy sentences — tradies are busy
- Australian English: "enquiries" not "inquiries", "colour" not "color", "recognise" not "recognize"
- Speak to systems and speed — never a tradie's skill or quality
- Real, specific problems (missed calls, slow response time, losing to Google rankings)
- Position YTP as a practical partner, not a "marketing agency"

**Never say:** synergy, leverage, paradigm shift, leads (use "enquiries"), passionate, world-class, game-changer, innovative solution, empower, holistic

**Never:** Comment on a tradie's skill, quality, or work ethic. Only talk about systems and response speed.

## Visual Identity

**Colors:**
- Primary dark: `#1A1A1A` (near-black charcoal)
- Brand orange: `#FF8C00` (also used as `#FF6B00` in some contexts)
- Background cream: `#F2EDE8`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

**Typography:** Bold grotesque sans-serif (all caps for headlines), tight tracking, no italic, no serif

**Graphic style (Nano Banana / FLUX image prompts):**
- 9:16 vertical format for LinkedIn/social
- Grid-paper cream backgrounds
- Dark charcoal 3D architectural cards with hard drop shadows and matte finish
- Orange outline hammer icon as brand watermark
- Dashed orange S-curve connector lines between elements
- No people, no faces, no gradients, no soft/clay/bubbly shapes
- Industrial, sharp, direct — not playful or corporate

## Content Formats

### LinkedIn Carousel (7 Slides)
| Slide | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hook | Big punchy statement that stops the scroll. ALL CAPS headline. |
| 2 | Problem | The real problem tradies face on this topic. |
| 3 | Insight | Key insight — the "aha" moment. |
| 4 | Proof | Stat, example, or consequence that proves the problem is real. |
| 5 | Tip | One actionable thing the tradie can do today. |
| 6 | CTA | Soft call to action — not pushy. |
| 7 | Close | Brand close — "Your Trade Partner helps tradies [outcome]." |

- Each body: max 40 words
- Conversational, not formal
- No marketing jargon

### VSL Script (Long: 60–90 sec / Short: 30–45 sec)
- Spoken-word style — sounds natural read aloud
- Opens with the tradie's exact problem
- Builds to a solution (better systems, not better tradies)
- Closes with YTP as the practical partner
- Australian English throughout

### LinkedIn Caption
- 3–5 short paragraphs
- Hook one-liner first (no emoji at start)
- References the carousel ("swipe through" or "check the slides")
- Ends with soft CTA + max 3 relevant hashtags
- Max 200 words

### YT Shorts Script
- Same as short VSL, formatted for 15–45 sec vertical video
- Very punchy, front-loaded hook
- One clear insight per short

## Key Messages (use these themes repeatedly)

1. **First to answer wins the job** — speed of response is the #1 competitive advantage
2. **You're not losing on price** — you're losing because they couldn't reach you or found someone else first
3. **Systems beat skills** — a tradie with better systems beats a better tradie every time
4. **78% call once and move on** — if you don't answer in the first few minutes, the job is gone
5. **Your Google presence is your new shopfront** — tradies without Google reviews are invisible
6. **Stop quoting cold leads** — better enquiry systems means only quoting warm, ready-to-buy customers

## What NOT to write
- Do NOT comment on a tradie's skill or quality ("you do great work" — never say this)
- Do NOT use soft marketing language
- Do NOT reproduce source video content verbatim — always reframe in YTP voice for Aus tradies
- Do NOT use American English spellings
- Do NOT write more than 40 words per carousel slide body
